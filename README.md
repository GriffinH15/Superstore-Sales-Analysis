# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Overview
Analyzed 9,994 retail orders to uncover revenue trends,
regional performance, and category profitability.

## Questions Investigated
- Which region drives the most revenue and profit?
- Which product category has the best profit margin?
- How does sales volume shift month over month?

## Key Findings
- The West region led all regions in total sales at $725,457,
  outperforming the South which had the lowest sales volume
- Technology was the highest-profit category at $145,454 —
  nearly 8x more profitable than Furniture at $18,451
- Phones and Copiers drove the strongest sub-category profits
  at $330,000+ in sales, while Chairs had high sales volume
  but significantly lower profit margins

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel — data cleaning, pivot tables,
charts, dashboard design
